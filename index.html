FAQ

You ask. We answer

You ask. We answer

(01)
How does Locbit work without internet?

Locbit uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to detect nearby devices and establish a direct peer-to-peer connection for messaging.
(02)
Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Every message is encrypted using AES-256-GCM, and keys are exchanged securely via ECDH protocols.
(03)
Does it consume a lot of battery?

No. Locbit is specifically engineered using BLE (Low Energy) to ensure minimal battery impact even when active.
(04)
Where is my message history stored?

All messages are stored locally on your device in structured JSON format. We never use central servers.
(05)
Who developed Locbit?

Locbit is an open-source project developed by Zulpikar Sandira and Ijal Gesrek (Drekeken-Tech).
